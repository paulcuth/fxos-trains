About

UK Trains is a tool to help navigate to your most-used pages on Accessible UK Train Timetables website. It is not affiliated with Accessible UK Train Timetables or National Rail.

Created by @paulcuth. Source available on GitHub.

Please report bugs and suggestions using the issue tracker, remembering to include the name of the device you are using.
